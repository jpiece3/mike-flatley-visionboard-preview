Budget Allocation Recommendations

Month 1: Test Phase

$500

  • Google Search: $200 (capture high-intent searches)
  • Facebook/Instagram: $300 (build awareness, test creatives)
  • Split test all 3 ad concepts
  • Track: Cost per lead, completion rate, lead quality

Month 2-3: Scale Phase

$1,000-$1,500

  • Double down on winning ad concept from Month 1
  • Add retargeting campaign for wishlist abandoners
  • Introduce video creative (30-second explainer)
  • Budget 60% to best performer, 40% to testing

Month 4+: Optimization Phase

$1,500-$2,000

  • Mature campaign with proven creatives
  • Add lookalike audiences based on past converters
  • Introduce seasonal angles (spring buying, holiday slow-down)
  • Monthly creative refresh to avoid ad fatigue

Targeting Strategy

Geographic Targeting

Primary Target

Chester County, PA (all zip codes)

Secondary Expansion

  • Montgomery County, PA (adjacent market)
  • Philadelphia, PA (relocators looking to move to suburbs)
  • Delaware County, PA (border communities)

Exclude: New Jersey, Delaware (outside service area)

Audience Segments

HOT Segment 1: Active Buyers (40% of budget)

  • Searching: "homes for sale chester county", "west chester real estate agent"
  • Facebook: Engaged with real estate pages in past 30 days

WARM Segment 2: Neighborhood Researchers (30% of budget)

  • Searching: "best neighborhoods chester county", "school districts pa"
  • Facebook: Interested in Chester County community pages

WARM Segment 3: First-Time Buyers (20% of budget)

  • Searching: "first time home buyer", "how to buy a house"
  • Facebook: Age 25-35, renting, recently engaged

COOL Segment 4: Relocators (10% of budget)

  • Searching: "moving to chester county", "philadelphia suburbs"
  • Facebook: Recent job change, new to area

A/B Tests to Run

  • Hero image (luxury home vs. diverse homes)
  • Social proof placement (above fold vs. below)

Monthly Reporting

What to Track

  1. Total spend by platform
  2. Leads generated by ad concept
  3. Cost per lead by audience segment
  4. Wishlist completion rate
  5. Lead temperature distribution (hot/warm/cool)
  6. Email engagement rates
  7. Scheduled appointments from vision board leads
  8. Closed deals attributed to vision board (long-term)

When to Pivot

  • If CPL >$25 for 2 weeks straight → pause and revise
  • If completion rate <40% → test new landing page copy
  • If hot lead % <5% → refine targeting or add qualification questions

Advanced Tactics (Month 6+)

  • Auto-rotate headlines/images based on performance
  • Facebook Dynamic Ads with property feeds
  • Build 1% lookalike of past closings
  • Build 1% lookalike of wishlist completers
  • Spring: "Spring Market Buyer's Guide"
  • Summer: "Before School Starts"
  • Fall: "Lock in Rates Before Year-End"
  • Winter: "New Year, New Home"
  • Co-market with local lenders (split ad cost)
  • Cross-promote on Chester County community pages
  • Sponsor local events with vision board booth

Copy & Creative Guidelines

Brand Voice

Voice: Approachable, strategic, no-pressure. Match The Gary Mercer Team's brand personality.

CTA: Action-oriented ("Build", "Create", "Find") not passive ("Learn More" unless Facebook forces it).

Visual Style

Visuals: Warm, inviting, aspirational but realistic. Avoid ultra-luxury if targeting first-time buyers.

Mobile: 60%+ of traffic is mobile. Design for thumb-scrolling.

Social Proof

Proof: Always include stats (401 sales, 563+ reviews) for credibility.

Legal & Compliance

  • All ads must comply with Fair Housing Act (no discriminatory language)
  • Equal Housing Opportunity logo on landing page
  • Privacy policy linked from email capture form
  • CAN-SPAM compliance for email sequences
  • Accurate representation of services (no misleading claims)

Questions for Optimization Calls

  1. Which ad concept is driving the highest completion rate?
  2. What's the cost per hot lead vs. cost per cool lead?
  3. Are certain neighborhoods or profiles converting better?
  4. What's the time-to-contact for hot leads?
  5. How many vision board leads turned into closings?

Summary

This ad strategy gives you 3 distinct angles (visual clarity, neighborhood matching, first-time buyer confidence) across 2 platforms (Google + Facebook/Instagram). Start at $500/month to test, scale to $2K/month once you find the winning formula.

The vision board builder is a top-of-funnel tool — prioritize volume and completion rate over immediate conversions. Long-term nurture will turn warm leads into closings.
