Content Creation Tips

Photography

  • Use high-quality images (hire photographer if needed)
  • Natural light, warm tones (match Soft design mode)
  • Mix professional listings with lifestyle shots
  • User-generated content when possible

Video

  • Keep Reels 15-30 seconds (algorithm prefers short)
  • Vertical format (9:16)
  • Captions/text overlay (most watch without sound)
  • Hook in first 3 seconds

Copy

  • Start with hook (question, stat, bold statement)
  • Bullet points for readability
  • One clear CTA per post
  • Conversational tone (match brand voice: strategic + fun)
