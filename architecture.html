Flow Design Rationale

  • Steps 1-2 (card_selection, card_selection): Visual, engaging, identity-forming. Step 1 uses large image cards, Step 2 uses medium icon cards -- visually distinct. The user starts by dreaming.
  • Step 3 (scale_selector): Quick, low-friction tap on a segmented bar. Breaks the card pattern. Practical but still about their life (family size, not finances).
  • Step 4 (chip_multi_select): The most expressive step. Multi-select chips let users build their wishlist. This is where micro-commitment peaks.
  • Steps 5-6 (scale_selector, card_selection): Practical, decision-oriented. Budget as a quiet scale, timeline as small cards. These are qualification dimensions but framed casually.

Selection type variety: 3 different types used (card_selection, scale_selector, chip_multi_select). No two consecutive steps use identical visual presentations.

Fallback Profile

Profile: The Nester
Rationale: The Nester represents the largest buyer segment in Chester County (growing families, 40-50% of traffic per research). When selections are too diffuse to clearly match a specific profile, they most likely fit the broad family-oriented buyer that Chester County primarily attracts.

Match Algorithm

For each profile:
  user_tags = all tags from user's selections across all 6 dimensions
  overlap = count(user_tags INTERSECT profile.trigger_tags)
  ratio = overlap / count(profile.trigger_tags)
  if ratio >= profile.match_threshold (0.5): candidate

Winner = candidate with highest ratio
Ties broken by profile order (first in array wins)
If no candidate exceeds threshold: fallback to The Nester

Composite Formula

composite = urgency * 0.6 + budget_fit * 0.4

Signal values: hot = 1.0, warm = 0.5, cool = 0.0

Composite thresholds:

  • >= 0.7 = Hot lead (immediate outreach, schedule showing)
  • 0.3 - 0.69 = Warm lead (nurture sequence, market updates)
  • < 0.3 = Cool lead (long-term drip, inspiration content)

Weight rationale: Urgency weighted higher (0.6) because real estate is timing-driven. The Gary Mercer Team's volume model (401 sales/year) means they can serve all budget levels -- urgency is the stronger signal.
