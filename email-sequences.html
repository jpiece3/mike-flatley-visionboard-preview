Email Design Notes:

  • Voice: Conversational, transparent, no-BS. Every email gives value before asking for anything.
  • CTAs: Primary CTA in every email. Secondary CTA where appropriate. Never more than 2 CTAs per email.
  • Personalization: Profile name and description used throughout. Dynamic content based on selections.
  • Unsubscribe Strategy: Explicitly mentioned in re-engagement emails to reduce list fatigue.
  • Mobile Optimization: Short paragraphs, bullet points, clear CTAs. Designed to be read on phones.
